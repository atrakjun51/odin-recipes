Calzone

Description

Calzones are basically an individual pizza all wrapped up, which means: no cheese sliding off, no floppy crusts, and completely portable. Pure deliciousness wrapped up in a pillowy dough, we'll take a calzone any day for an easy weeknight dinner or as a fun lunch we're actually excited to eat. Stuffed with melty cheese and pepperoni, this easy recipe gives calzones their much-needed due attention.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 3/4 lb. pizza dough
  • All-purpose flour, for surface
  • 1 c. pizza sauce
  • 1 c. ricotta
  • 1/2 c. pepperoni
  • 1 c. shredded mozzarella
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, for brushing
  • Kosher salt
